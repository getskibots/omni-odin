 How to analyse and improve AI Knowledge replies? How to analyse and improve AI Knowledge replies? 
 AI Knowledge replies sub-tab of the NLP tab can provide useful insights about your knowledge base performance. You can validate correctness of the replies based on the provided data and improve replies for future similar questions from users. 
 Here you can see: Questions sent by users Replies generated by AI knowledge Link to conversation with specific reply Link to source of the specific reply Timestamp of the reply Pencil icon with ability to create new text source from the reply 
 Data can be filtered by date and language. 

 🟢 - the source is text sample, click on the source will redirect you to specific text sample in the AI knowledge tab 🟠 - the source is website, click on the source will redirect you to specific webpage 🔵 - the source is file, click on the source will open link to pdf in browser or download txt/doc file to device 🟡 - the answer is based not on trained sources, but on the custom prompt info and/or general GPT knowledge 
 How to correct replies? Click on pencil icon near reply you want to improve The “Improve AI knowledge” modal view will be opened The title and text in the view is automatically pre-filled with user’s question and AI answer Edit answer and title Press “Add to knowledge” Edited text will be added to the bot's knowledge, improving its ability to answer similar questions in the future. This means that new text source will be created in the AI knowledge sub tab.
