Introduction Home page: sharing a chatbot Bot Settings page: sharing a bot Invitation to join Enterprise Platform Related articles Introduction You can share a chatbot with your colleagues in two ways: from the Home page or the Bot Settings page. Learn about both of them below. Home page: sharing a chatbot Follow these steps if you need to grant access to multiple chatbots. This way you will save extra time. So, once you are on the Home page: Find a chatbot you want to share, Press the icon with 3 dots → and then on 'Share'. The following popup will appear: In the popup, provide new user's email address and choose the role: ‘Simple Admin’, ‘Admin’ or ‘Support’. Press the 'Share' button to finish. Bot Settings page: sharing a bot Here's another way of sharing a bot with a colleague: Navigate to the Settings page → Users. Click on the 'Invite new user' button. A popup will appear: insert the user's email address and select an appropriate role. Click on the 'Share' button to send the invitation. Check the 'Send notifications' box, if you want that your user receives email notifications on new support requests (recommended for users with Support role). 
 Invitation to join Enterprise Platform That's it! In both cases, the system will send an invitation to the email address you provided in the sharing settings. A new user will be able to register and access the shared bot via the Home page. An existing user will just see the new bot appear on their Home page.
