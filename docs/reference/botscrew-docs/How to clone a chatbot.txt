Follow these steps if you need to create a copy of an existing chatbot with all its flows and settings. Might be very useful, if you want to have a similar or slightly adjusted chatbot on different platforms. Cloning a chatbot Log in to your Enterprise Platform and navigate to the Home page. Find the chatbot you want to clone and click on the three dots button. Tap on the 'Clone' button in the drop-down. Enter the name of your clone's chatbot (make sure it's unique). Click on 'Clone' to finish. 
 ☝🏽Note that NLP content is not cloned. To create a copy of the bot with NLP content use Export/Import . Congratulations! All done! Proceed to adjusting or using your cloned chatbot. 🖖 Related articles Home page How to transfer chatbot’s content between bots?
