Check the following articles: Introduction Before sharing your chatbot with real users it's better to have it tested. In this article, we will tell you how to do it. Test in Messenger Test in Messenger option is available if you have already connected your chatbot with a Facebook page . If this is the case, simply follow these steps: 
 Tap on the 'Test in Messenger' button in the top right-hand corner of the screen. It's available from any tab. Facebook Messenger will open in a new tab. Follow your designed flow to see if all atoms are well connected and if the replies are sent accordingly: If you notice any issues — navigate to Enterprise Platform, Flows tab to see if any atoms are left unlinked. Test in Widget 
 Tab on the 'Test in widget' button in the top right-hand corner of the screen. It's available from any tab. The widget is in the bottom right-hand corner. Click on it to open. Provide the required details, if any, and click on 'Start'. 
 Test your chatbot flow as if you were a real user. 5. If you see that some changes are required, jump into Enterprise Platform → Flows tab to adjust your chatbot flow. Congratulation!
