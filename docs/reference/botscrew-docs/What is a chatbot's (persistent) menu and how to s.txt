Introduction Introduction The main (persistent) menu allows users to access the core functionality of your bot (e.g. Customer Support, Help, FAQ, and others) from the side menu in the corner of the chat. In order to implement it for your widget, follow these simple steps: Navigate to the Settings page → Main Menu: Click at the "+ Click here to add a menu item", Popup for creating menu is opened: Enter the name of the menu: Choose 'Postback' or 'URL' options ( Postback redirects a user to the next atom, and URL redirects them to the link) Once done, click on "Save" button: Item is saved and displayed on the list: In the result you will get this menu:
